Esbjerg Amatør Teater

Bringing magical stories to life in Esbjerg since 1998.

Our Mission

In Esbjerg Amatør Teater – Skarntyden we do two major performances every year. One is a performance for children, which is typically during the winter holidays, and the other is a free performance in connection with Esbjerg Festuge. In between we also have other activities.

Our Values

What guides everything we do

Passion for stories

We believe in the transformative power of storytelling to inspire, educate, and bvring communities together

Community First

Skarntyden exists to serve the families of Esbjerg and beyond, creating accesible cultural experiences for all.

Artistic Excellence

We are committed to producing high-quality theatre that honors both classic tales and contemporary voices.

Youth Empowerment

Our program nurture young talent and foster a lifelong love of the performing arts in the next generation.
