The Story of the Brothers Lionheart

A tale of courage, love, and adventure

Karl is sick, very sick. His older brother Jonathan tells him stories of Nangiyala, a magical land where they will one day be reunited. When Jonathan dies saving Karl from a fire, Karl must find his way to this mystical place where his brave brother awaits.

In the land of campfires and sagas, the brothers live in Cherry Valley, a paradise of freedom and joy. But peace is threatened by the tyrant Tengil and his fearsome dragon Katla, who rule the neighboring land of Karmanjaka with an iron fist.

When Cherry Valley is threatened, Jonathan joins the resistance, and Karl must overcome his fears to fight alongside his brother. Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know for your visit

Tickets & Bookings

How do I purchase tickets?

Tickets can be purchased online through our official website or at the box office.

Can I choose my seats?

Yes, you can choose your seats during the online booking process, subject to availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations are permitted up to 24 hours before the performance, subject to a booking fee. Please check our full policy for details.

Are group discounts available?

Group discounts are available for parties of 10 or more. Please contact our group bookings office for pricing.

Arrival & Practicalities

When should we arrive?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start time to allow for seating and any pre-show purchases.

Is there parking available?

Limited street parking is available, and there are several paid parking garages within a two-block radius.

Can I bring food or drinks into the theatre?

Outside food and drinks are generally not permitted. Concessions are available for purchase inside the theatre.

Is the theatre wheelchair accessible?

Yes, the theatre is fully wheelchair accessible. Please inform the box office when booking so we can allocate the most suitable seating.
